About Us

Established in the year 2008, Zeus India Enterprise is a Bangalore (Karnataka) based Manufacturer, Exporter and Supplier of Natural Mango Pulp. We are having office at Bangalore and own manufacturing facility in Krishnagiri (dt), Tamil Nadu. As per last fiscal year’s report, we have managed to scale annual turnover of INR 5 Crores in a short span of time which testifies our commendable performance. We produce Mango pulp in cans as per customers advance orders & market requirements.


Infrastructural Facilities

Production Facilities :Spread over a sprawling area of 15,000 square feet, our processing unit is outfitted with automated fruit washer, tip cutting conveyer, destoner, pulper, finisher, pre-heater, centrifuge decanter, kettles, strainers, magnetic separators and canning equipment. Subsequently, we have extended our production capacity to 3.0 M.T of finished goods per hour.

Production Capacity : 4.0 M.T / Finished Product /Hour.

Testing Facilities : We have in-house testing facility whereby processed Mango Pulp is duly tested to ensure taste, composition, aroma, shelf-life, quality, packaging and other aspects before final shipment of consignments at buyers’ end. In house Laboratory to test online Products for every batch to standardize the final product as per International Standards.

Packaging & Storage Facilities : We use highly reliable, compatible and weatherproof packaging material as per dictated packaging guidelines for Pulp Products. The storage area has restricted entry in order to minimize risk of unfavorable impurities and preserve product-quality for long period of time.


Quality Assurance:

The variety of Mango Pulp we deal in is assured with the high-grades of quality as only natural mangoes are used in manufacturing the Fresh Mango Pulp. Furthermore, team of expert quality analysts and production managers keep an eye on each stage of procurement process to final packaging of products.
